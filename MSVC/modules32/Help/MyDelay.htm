My Delay

This is a simple wrapper around AudioKitCore::AdjustableDelayLine. It provides a basic delay effect with adjustable delay time up to 1.0 sec.

Pins

  • Input
  • DelaySec Delay time (seconds, 0.0 to 1.0)
  • DryFrac Dry fraction (0.0 to 1.0)
  • Feedback Feedback amount (0.0 to 1.0)
  • Output
