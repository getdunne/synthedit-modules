Soundpipe Phaser

This is a simple wrapper around the sp_phaser module from Soundpipe. It provides a stereo-in, stereo-out phase-shifter effect.

Pins

  • Input L
  • Input R
  • MaxNotch1Freq Between 20 and 10000 (Default value: 800)
  • MinNotch1Freq Between 20 and 5000 (Default value: 100)
  • NotchWidth Between 10 and 5000 (Default value: 1000)
  • NotchFreq Between 1.1 and 4 (Default value: 1.5)
  • VibratoMode 1 or 0 (Default value: 1)
  • Depth Between 0.0 and 1.0
  • Feedback Between 0.0 and 1.0 (Default value: 0.0)
  • Invert -60 to 10 dB (Default value: 0)
  • Level Dry fraction (0.0 to 1.0)
  • LFO_BPM Between 24 and 360 (Default value: 30)
  • Output L
  • Output R
